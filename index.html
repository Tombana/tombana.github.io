Tom Bannink

I am a deep learning scientist at Plumerai, studying binarized neural networks. I did a PhD at CWI and QuSoft under the supervision of Harry Buhrman and Frank den Hollander. I received my bachelor degrees in mathematics and physics at Utrecht University (2013) and a masters degree in theoretical physics (2015). My PhD research focussed on quantum information and in particular quantum walks, a quantuntum mechanical analogue of classical random walks.
